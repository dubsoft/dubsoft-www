Tempus Time Tracker

Tempus is a time tracker that is changing the way people track time. The idea is to leverage the power of data, intelligent scripts and programs, and natural language processing to track/record/export time with minimal effort.

Tempus is an alpha product and is always looking for user/potential user input. Head on over to www.t3mp.us to join the mailing list and to be a beta tester!

For the software developer type, Tempus is an open source project licensed under the BSD 3 Clause license. Feel free to look at the source code and provide feedback and contributions!
